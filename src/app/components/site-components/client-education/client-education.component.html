What is Financial Futures.

Finanacial Futures is a contract between Reserves Manager Limited, seller and our Client, buyer. that we sell our Product now, example specific future House Rent Value, which we call Future Cash Value at a Price, which we call the Present Cash Value. The future date, Tenure for each contract start from three months and could be along as our Clents require.

Our Futures Products.

  • House Rent.
  • School Fees.
  • Value for Holiday Making.
  • Values for Hajj and Umrah.
  • Value for Festivities and Socialisation.
  • Business Ventures, Salaries, Wages, Overhead and etc.
  • Debt Payments.
  • Projects Funding.
  • General Reserves.
  • Others as Clients Require.

In today's World, Unemployment, Job Insecurity, Busuness Uncertainty and Failures, Unreliable Governments and Bad Policies, and high cost of borrowing funds, it is very neceesary for Individual and Corporate Entities to make adeguate plans in other to create adequate cah liquidities day to day financial obligations.

Our Futures Products Return Types.

  • Fixed Return.
  • Variable Return.
  • Withdrawing Variable Return.

The Price, Present Cash Value for each category depend on both the Future Cash Value and Tenure, the longer the Tenure the lower the Present Cash Value.

Example.

  • Client who require a Future Cash Value of 600,000.00 Nigeria Naira for House Rent in 6 months from now, shall pay 200,000 Naira now as Present Cash Value.
  • Client who require a Future Cash Value of 600,000.00 Nigeria Naira for House Rent in 12 months from now, shall pay 70,000.00 Naira now as Present Cash Value.
  • Client who require a Future Value of 600,000.00 Nigeria Naira for House Rent in 24 months from now, shall pay 30,000.00 Naira now as Present Cash Value.

Fixed Return. The return, Future Cash Value is determined upfront. The return is what the customer get on due date. Variable Return. The return, Future Cash Value is not determined upfront. The return depend our Trade Performance for the futures tenure. Monthly Statements are sent to the customer to show monthly performances and all our service fees and Commissions charges. Withdrawing is not allowed until end of tenure. Variable Withdrawing Return. Is like Variable Return, but withdrawing is allowed after 3months. However Customer cannot withdraw to bring the Equity bellow the Present Cash Value.
