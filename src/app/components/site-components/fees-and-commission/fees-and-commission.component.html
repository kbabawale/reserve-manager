Fees and Commission

Service Fees & Commission Percentage Rates, Service Fees

We charge percentage of wealth we create for as service Fees and the rate changes progressively at end of month service fees are calculated as follows, example: A client invest ₦400,000

  • 10% Flat of wealth we create at 100%
  • 20% Flat of wealth we create above 100%
  • 30% Flat of wealth we create above 200%
  • 40% Flat of wealth we create above 300%
  • 50% Flat of wealth we create above 400%

  • At end of month 1, our total service Fees = ₦20,000
